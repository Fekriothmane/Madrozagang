Territory & Turf

Control over territory means control over resources and influence. Madrazo rules multiple neighborhoods and trade routes in Los Santos.

Our turf is divided into zones secured by trusted lieutenants, with active patrols and lookout posts.

Main Turf Areas

  • Red Garage District: The heart of our operations and the site of recent turf wars.
  • East Los Santos: Hub for supply routes and front businesses.
  • Downtown Industrial Zone: Chop shops and vehicle storage.

Rival Territories

We are in constant conflict with:

  • Marabunta Grande: Known for ruthless territorial expansion.
  • Lost MC: Control of illegal weapons supply.
