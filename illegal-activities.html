Illegal Activities

To rule the streets, you gotta control the shadows. Here’s a breakdown of Madrazo’s operations that keep us on top:

Drug Trafficking

We handle the wholesale and street-level distribution of narcotics throughout Los Santos. Our network ensures product quality and supply chain security with ruthless enforcement.

Weapons Dealing

From pistols to heavy artillery, Madrazo sources and sells firearms to trusted affiliates and personal enforcers. We stay armed and dangerous.

Vehicle Theft & Chop Shops

High-end cars and utility vehicles alike are snatched, dismantled, and sold or repurposed to fund our operations and keep our members mobile and unpredictable.

Money Laundering

Dirty cash flows through front businesses and underground banks, cleaned meticulously by our money laundering specialists, maintaining financial dominance and secrecy.

Robberies & Heists

From small-time stores to high-value bank hits, the Madrazo gang has perfected the art of robbery, combining strategy, speed, and brutality.

Every illegal move is calculated, executed, and controlled. That’s how empires are built, and ours is unshakable.
