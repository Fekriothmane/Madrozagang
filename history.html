The Madrazo Legacy

Founded in 1999 by the legendary El Padrino Madrazo, the gang began as a small crew of street runners and hustlers determined to take control of Los Santos’s underground.

Through grit, strategy, and a strict code of loyalty, the Madrazo Gang grew into a formidable empire, feared by rivals and respected by allies. The gang’s rise was marked by bold heists, brutal turf wars, and unshakable brotherhood.

Key Moments

  • 1999: Formation of Madrazo Gang by El Padrino and his closest confidants.
  • 2004: The Vinewood Bank Heist, putting Madrazo on the map.
  • 2015: Expansion into legitimate businesses and front operations.
  • 2022: Turf war against Marabunta Grande, solidifying control of Red Garage District.

Today, the Madrazo Gang stands as a symbol of power and legacy — a family bound not by blood, but by respect, fear, and the shared pursuit of dominion.
