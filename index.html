Welcome to the Madrazo Gang

Born in the streets, raised by the code of loyalty and power, the Madrazo Gang reigns supreme in Los Santos. Our empire is built on respect, fear, and unbreakable bonds. We control the city’s darkest corners and run operations that keep the cash flowing and rivals trembling.

From the blood-stained alleys to the neon-lit nightclubs, Madrazo is more than a gang — it’s a legacy. Our members live by honor, and our enemies learn to fear the name. Dive into our world to discover the players, the business, and the wars that shape the streets.

Join us as we expand our territory, sharpen our edge, and carve our name into the city’s history forever.
