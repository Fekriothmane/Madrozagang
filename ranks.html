Madrazo Gang Ranks & Hierarchy

Our organization thrives on order and respect. Each rank carries responsibility, trust, and the weight of loyalty.

Hierarchy

  • El Padrino (Boss): The ultimate authority. Commands respect and controls all operations.
  • Consigliere: The advisor and strategist. Keeps the boss informed and mediates disputes.
  • Underboss: Second in command. Oversees day-to-day operations and enforcers.
  • Caporegime (Captain): Leads squads of soldiers. Executes orders and manages territory.
  • Enforcer: Muscle of the gang. Handles security, intimidation, and enforcement.
  • Dealer: Responsible for narcotics distribution and street-level operations.
  • Soldier: Full member with all rights and duties.
  • Prospect: A probationary member proving loyalty and worth.

Promotion is earned through loyalty, bravery, and contribution to the family’s power.
